About Brain Tumor Detection

Brain Tumor Detection is an application designed to assist in the diagnosis and classification of brain tumors using artificial intelligence.

Types of Brain Tumors

Glioma

Gliomas are tumors that originate in the glial cells of the brain. They can be either benign or malignant and can occur in different parts of the brain.

Meningioma

Meningiomas are tumors that develop in the meninges, the protective membranes that surround the brain and spinal cord. They are usually benign and slow-growing.

Pituitary Tumor

Pituitary tumors are growths that develop in the pituitary gland, a small gland located at the base of the brain. They can cause hormonal imbalances and affect various bodily functions.

No Tumor

Not all brain abnormalities are tumors. Some conditions may mimic the appearance of tumors on scans, but they are not characterized by abnormal cell growth.
